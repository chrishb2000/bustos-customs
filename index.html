About Us

Professional Cabinet Makers in Dallas, TX

Bustos Custom Cabinets & Millwork is a professional cabinet making company with extensive experience in home remodeling. We specialize in all types of custom woodwork and laminates, bringing your vision to life with precision and quality.

From consulting on design to building cabinets using the best materials, and follow-up on every detail after installation, our team is dedicated to delivering top-notch craftsmanship. We work with 3D drawings and views to help you visualize your project before we begin.
